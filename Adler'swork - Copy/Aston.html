Once upon a time,there lived a boy namedAston. Aston lived in Kenya in Nairobi capital city. He had a sister named Arianah.One day they were going to the market a speeding car came and crashed into them and they were both taken to the hospital Aston broke all his limbs and Arianah broke her wrist From that day on they learned to look left and right when crossing a road.
